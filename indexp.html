Review of: Cubbon Park

Garden City

It's rare to have a sweet old beloved park in the middle of any city, but for Bangalore it's a kind of signal place for what makes the rest of the city so clean and so green. A morning walk here tells you so much about Bangalore's priorities - which I hope are not already "erstwhile.".
